# GitHub Onboarding: What This Work Shows

## What Do I Do?

I build and document operational governance infrastructure for AI-enabled systems, with a focus on runtime oversight, governance telemetry, decision boundaries, escalation logic, Stop Authority, and longitudinal accountability.

## What Problems Do I Solve?

I help make AI governance observable during execution.

Most organizations have policies, principles, or high-level frameworks. The gap is proving what actually happened while systems were running.

This work focuses on:
- operational AI oversight
- governance documentation
- runtime risk visibility
- process integrity
- evidence continuity
- AI-human workflow provenance
- replayable governance traces

## Why Does This Matter Operationally?

AI governance cannot remain only policy language.

Once AI systems act inside real workflows, organizations need evidence of:
- who made decisions
- what boundaries existed
- whether escalation was available
- whether Stop Authority existed
- what the system did during execution
- whether behavior can be reconstructed afterward

This repository documents the professional transition from independent systems research into operational AI governance, process analysis, and runtime oversight.

## Core Principle

Time turns behavior into infrastructure.  
Behavior is the most honest data there is.
